Conclusion

It gets a little bit clumsy when you go deeper, i mean when you start nesting all of these div's.

i wanna mention that if i'm intrested to make two ancestor divs the same height, i'll focus on the smallest one then i'll apply grid on it, in other words display: flex, btw the parent should be grid in the first place!

So, when you specify one div to display: flex he gonna automatically stretch himself to the height of his encounter "the div that's on his left/right".
